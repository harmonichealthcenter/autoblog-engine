# Question-Based Article Type

Question-based articles target single specific questions. They're optimized for featured snippets, Google's "People Also Ask", and AI-assistant citations.

## Structure expectations
- 1,200 to 1,500 words.
- 4-6 H2 sections, each phrased as a related sub-question.
- Open with a 40-60 word direct answer to the question — this is the snippet target. No preamble. Just answer it.
- Then expand: context, nuance, examples, "but what if", related questions.
- Use H2/H3 questions that mirror real "People Also Ask" phrasing (use natural conversational language, not stiff SEO-speak).
- Closing CTA uses "RESONANCE" once.

## Question-specific guidance
- The opening answer paragraph is critical. It should be quotable, accurate, and 40-60 words. Read it back and ask: "If Google or ChatGPT pulled this exact paragraph, would I be proud of it?"
- Treat skeptical questions ("is Qiari legit", "does remote frequency wellness actually work") with respect. Don't dismiss the skepticism or oversell. Address the question honestly, frame Qiari as wellness-experience-not-medical-treatment, point out what's verifiable (subscriber experiences, structured app design) without overclaiming what's not (clinical efficacy).
- For "is X legit" type queries especially, a defensive or salesy tone tanks credibility. Calm and direct wins.
